Net Pots/Propagation Tubes


  • Our smart net pots or tubes designs are suitable for propagation nurseries where growers can bury underground and carry out repotting to larger pots directly. This design not only saves labour, management cost, time but also achieve better growing results. Brilliant open side slits designs can let plants grow quicker and easier to monitor growth. More importantly prevent root damage during repotting.
  • Various formulas to suit different kinds of young plants, vary from below 1 month to 1 year.

Direct repotting to other pots

  • Field planting with net pots can be done under most weather conditions without hurting the roots or affect growth. From photos below can see the comparison of young plant bury underground with net pots and the one removed from conventional plastic pots.
