About Vipot

Faced with ever growing pollution and diminishing resources on Earth, growers, retailers and consumers have been looking for an environmentally friendly solution that is affordable and reliable. We hope that one day our products will be able to replace synthetic plastics altogether.
Raw Materials

The novel feature that makes Vipot products nature friendly is its composition. It is primarily made up of sustainable natural plant fibre obtained from the wastes of widely consumed crops; mainly rice husk. The remaining material binds the rice husk together, which is both synthetic and non-toxic.
